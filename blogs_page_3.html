Did you suck yourself ?

Have you ever felt that your efforts and hard work go unnoticed?

Do you have the confusion of why your growth stocked in the same phase?

Have you ever felt like you are sucked by somebody?

Are you the one who pauses a second and feel like oh that's me when you came across the phrase "The Life sucks"?

Are you the one who tired of being everything to everyone?

At some point do you feel like your actions went meaningless?



These sets of interrogations will rise up when you let somebody suck you and not your own self or when you fail to say no to the things which really deserves no in your life.



Here is the tiny magic to be filled within the fragile 175 pounds of weight which reminds you every second that you are a celebration to the mother earth. Yes, the ..... SELF LOVE!



Have you ever adored yourself?

When is the last time you have made thumbs up to yourself?

When is the last time you took the time to appreciate your run to come this far?

Have you ever celebrated yourself?

Have you ever embraced the pupil in your eyes, the pimple in your cheeks, the smile on your lips, the soothe of your eyelashes, the pattern of your hair, the design of your ear, the acuteness of your nose, the story behind your scars, the war behind the stitches, the sorting of your tooth, the eccentric lines in your palm, the templates of your nails, the stunning complexion of your skin, the curves in your anatomy?

"Your soul meant to be overflown and not to be sucked".

Aren't you the person to be overflown?



The truth is that life doesn't suck anyone until you fail to suck yourself...

Suck your quality time to grow...

Suck your relentless efforts to achieve your goals...

Suck your untiring potential to chase your dreams...

Suck your barriers to cross the storms...

Suck your love and care to nourish your own self...



Sucking yourself never means being selfish but replenishing your self with the essence called self-love! Remember,



"You are not a glass of juice to be sucked, but a barrel of wine to be overflown!"

Once you learn to suck yourself there is no space for others to suck you because you are now an overflowing barrel in the midst of the vineyard.



I wish your barrel to be filled with the most delicious essence of all!
