I grow with you everyday

When the warmth of the night sky was only the reason for me to stay alive, You came as a dusky, dark and stardust seed craved to thrive;

I replanted myself besides planting you, So that you and me will start to germinate new;

I was worried that we were buried, In the genesis of battle;

As I slowly build foundation strong with love and patience, and started visionizing two rays of hope in you that was time and persistence;

I guarded you with the fence, as like I guard my heart from negative sense;

You taught me what comes later is the fruit of the foundation and fencing, And now you have grown bigger than something meaningful that made me dancing;

You gifted me your first flower, Finally I saw my ownself into yours; Am grateful for you all-day ,

for growing with you everyday!
