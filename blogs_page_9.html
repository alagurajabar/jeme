Finding Joy in the midst of Chaos

Never tired Appa and his old scooter waits at the bus stop watching keenly for your bus; Gets all the luggage and the burden in your heart that you carry all the long way.

Area Watchman uncle who smiles at you and always asks How are you kanna? whenever you go home and help Appa to carry your luggage to home;

The neighbourhood kid who run towards you with the purest form of love and laugh and hug your knees tight and ask akka what you brought for me this time?

Selfless Amma who waits all day long with sleepless night and waits at entrance gate to pamper you with warm hugs to symbolise "Everything will be okay my child";

Most annoying brother who wakes up with the sleepy face and blabber with the half sleep and say that Hey don't eat my cookies and Lays inside the fridge okay;

But brought everything just for you before the day you arrive home;

Silver crowned granny who give warm kisses on your forehead with her toothless smile and the chocolates she saved for you from your brother in her medicine box;

All the plants in garden that Amma nurtured will dance along with chipping birds medley to just say that they missed you and love you!

Friend who just plan for a road trip without your permission whenever she knew your arrival and comes home with the scooter to rush you to get ready for the same;

Whenever you go to terrace for gazing, the Sun starts to paint a beautiful picture every morning and the Moon starts to vanish all your sorrows with light from the beautiful people around!

And finally!

It's absolutely not the things that we thought before could be the reason anymore for the real JOY but the lil ones that we missed to notice many times !



Isn't a beautiful reason to the real happiness ?
